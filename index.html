Welcome to the Magic Forest

Deep inside the Magic Forest live tiny fairies, giggling mushrooms, and twinkling stars. Today you're a Sparkle Catcher — a gentle helper who finds shy fairies and gives them a tiny flower to say hello. As you click fairies and sparkles, you'll collect points and learn fun forest secrets!

Meet the Friends

Faye the Fairy
Likes sparkles and singing to the moon.
Milo the Mushroom
A shy mushroom who pops up for a game.
Twinkle Star
A tiny star who loves to dance in the trees.

How to Play

  1. Press Start to begin the 30 second round.
  2. Click or press Enter/Space on fairies and sparkles to collect them.
  3. Fairies give more points than mushrooms — try to catch the fairies!
  4. When time's up, you'll see your score. Try again to beat it!
